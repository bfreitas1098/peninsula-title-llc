Closing Services

Peninsula Title manages the closing of a transaction from beginning to end, offering these closing services:

  • Managing the secure holding of funds and documents during the closing process, ensuring that all conditions are met before disbursement.
  • Assisting in the preparation and review of legal documents essential to the closing, including deeds, closing statements, and any required disclosures.
  • Facilitating the transfer of ownership by coordinating the signing of necessary documents, verifying the legal transfer of title, and ensuring compliance with all legal requirements.
  • Handling the submission and recording of necessary documents with the appropriate government authorities, such as deeds and mortgage instruments, to formalize the transfer of ownership.
  • Coordinating the release of funds from escrow to the appropriate parties, including sellers, real estate agents, and any other entities involved in the transaction, in accordance with the terms of the agreement.
