Frequently Asked Questions

What is Title?
A title is legal ownership and rights to a property.
What is Title Insurance?
Title insurance is a policy that protects property buyers and lenders from potential legal issues or defects in the property's ownership history.
What is a Title Search?
A title search is a standard part of the home buying process that is conducted to uncover issues that could prevent your right to the property. Your closing agent reviews public records to see if there are problems or defects that could cause you legal issues.
Why Should I Purchase Owner's Title Insurance?
Purchasing title insurance provides protection against potential legal issues or disputes related to a property's ownership, ensuring peace of mind for the buyer and lender.
How Much Does Owner's Title Insurance Cost?
The one-time payment for owner's title insurance is a fixed rate dependent on the sales price. Typically it costs around 0.5% of the home's purchase price.
How Long Am I Covered?
Your owner's insurance policy lasts as long as you own your property.
What Happens at Closing?
You sign the legal documents and receive the keys to your home.
